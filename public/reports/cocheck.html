Coronavirus: do your Co-Check!

Question Title

* I always wash my hands (before having a meal, after going out, after every toilet visit, etc.).

Because regular hand washing is the most effective measure to slow down the transmission of the coronavirus.

Question Title

* I avoid touching my face.

Because by touching your eyes, mouth or nose the risk of introducing the virus via the mucous membranes is considerable. I try to become aware of every time I touch my face and gradually decrease the frequency. I share this concern with those with whom I live.

Question Title

* I regularly clean my living space, my personal belongings (phone…), and my workspace.

Because the virus survives on objects for up to three days. I try to become aware of the objects I touch, especially after I have been out and been in contact with others or with objects that others may have touched recently .

Question Title

* When greeting someone, I avoid physical contact.

Because the virus survives for several hours on your body and clothes.

Question Title

* I keep a distance of 2 meters from others.

Because the virus is transported in the air by more or less fine droplets.

Question Title

* I limit my visits to high risk and crowded places such as hospitals, shops, airports.

Because the risk of being infected and infecting others is higher.

Question Title

* I cough and sneeze in the fold of my arm.

To avoid infecting others in case I am infected.

Question Title

* I wear a mask in places where I cannot keep physical distance (example: public transport).

Because by wearing a mask I avoid infecting others and reduce my risk of being infected.

Question Title

* I care for everyone without prejudice.

Because discrimination slows down our collective resistance to the coronavirus, I maintain the same respectful attitude towards everyone.

Question Title

* I am extra careful when meeting with older and vulnerable persons.

Because the elderly and people with multiple illnesses have a higher risk of dying from coronavirus.

Question Title

* I take care of myself in order to care for others.

Because I am aware that my own physical, emotional and mental health affects the well-being of others.

Question Title

* I use this pandemic as an opportunity to improve the quality of life.

Because the coronavirus reveals the fragility of our society, I review my relationship with myself, with others and with nature to contribute to the construction of a more sustainable society.
