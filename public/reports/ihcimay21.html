Wayanad District IHCI Monthly Report
May 2021


Registrations till May 2021: 25,707


Registrations across Facility Type: Types = SDH, CHC & PHC

The reason for lesser registrations at the SDH and CHC is that these were converted into COVID-19 First Line Treatment Centers or full-fledged COVID-19 designated centers, and so no NCD clinics are held in these facilities.

However, strikes by doctors and Health staff has brought documentation to a halt across most facilities. Also, resurgnece of COVID-19, is cutting down the little improvements in new registration.
