Wayanad District IHCI Monthly Report
Dec 2020


Registrations till Dec 2020: 24,298


Registrations across Facility Type: Types = SDH, FHC & PHC

The reason for lesser registrations at the SDH and CHC is that these were converted into COVID-19 First Line Treatment Centers or full-fledged COVID-19 designated centers, and so no NCD clinics were held in these facilities.

However, new advisories from the State restarted the NCD program at these facilities with due precautions to reduce COVID-19 transmission. Accordingly, an upward trend in registration is expected. Also, the rising trend in PHCs for the last three months is encouraging.

Achievements, Recommendations

  1. The upward trend seen in new Registration which started in Nov 2020 has remained static at 139 for Dec 2020 as well.
  2. With more patients visiting facilities the NCD drugs distributed to the patients at their houses, will reduce in the next few months.
  3. However, as most patients still feared transmission of COVID-19, the trend of doorstep delivery will most likely continue.
  4. More Tribal Mobile Medical Units could cater to the 22% Tribal populace.


High & Low Performers

  • The highest registration for Dec 2020 was of 60 hypertensives from THQH Bathery
  • 26 facilities recorded zero registrations.

    If there is a reduction in the number of COVID-19 cases, then those CHC and SDH, which have been designated as COVID-19 hospitals or FLTC, could be converted back to regular hospitals, and the NCD clinics in them restarted.
  • NCD clinics most likely to restart by mid or end Jan 2021. Directive form the State authorities to this effect is expected in Mid-Jan.

What is Stock in Patient Days?

The Stock in Patient Days or Stock in Days(SID) is the number of days that the present stock of the protocol drug would last for the number of patients registered in the program.
