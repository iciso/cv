Donations

The Thanjavur Medical Journal (TMJ) is owned by the Thanjavur Medical College Alumni Association (TMCAA) Trust Board. The Thanjavur Medical Journal is published by the TMC Alumni (ALM) Publications Trust. The ALM Publication Trust has applied for seed funding to the Trust Board of the TMC Alumni Associaton.

Author Fees

The TMJ intends to collect a small token fee from authors to cover Peer Reviewer costs exclusively. Total fees thus collected will not exceed 1% of the forecasted budget, which is mainly met by seed funding as an unsecured interest free loan.

TMC Alumni (ALM) Publications Trust

The first objective of the TMC Alumni Publications Trust is to improve the quality of recording, documenting, reviewing, editing, and publishing the best medical, clinical, and health articles, and the best science from the best of TMC Alumni in the state, nation, and worldwide, so as to provide a far global reach and more positive impact on health universally. .

TMJ Publication Program & other Services

The Thanjavur Medical Journal (TMJ) invites original submissions for peer review consideration on any health theme. These themes will be explored from our inaugural issue onwards. As for other services, the TMC Alumni Publications Trust will take up CME, Training and other Medical Education supporting role through Research and Data studies.

Knowledge Medical Products

The TMC Journal Website intends to become a site that the TMC online community visits frequently to use the knowledge products, in order to better engage with their patients. Thus, the Journal website would be a local site with unique knowledge products that have global content and universal health messages.

Viable & Sustainable

The active participation of every faculty and student currently in TMC ensures the sustainability of TMJ. The issue of reluctance to publish in a non-indexed journal would apply in the initial days. Alternatively, the Journal gets indexed only if we have quality publications. Also, quality manuscripts will come in only if the journal is indexed.
